What is Aila?

This bot is mostly a hobby of mine, I have crazy ideas of turning it into something much bigger than a Discord bot. But for now, I just try to make it different from others.

I started developing bots using Ruby around the middle of 2016. This technically has some of the original code, but it's been iterated on so many times by now, you can't tell. As such, this bot is pretty much my biggest and most advanced project.


Features
Neural Network AI Beta

Based on the same engine behind the Replika app by the Luakalabs Team. Aila's AI "learns" as you speak with her. She also understands context to some degree and has emtion tagging (Use emojis to clarify your intent or mood.)
You can learn more about how the engine works at cakechat.replika.ai
